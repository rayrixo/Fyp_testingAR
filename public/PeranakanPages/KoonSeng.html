Koon Seng Road Peranakan Shophouses

Koon Seng Road was known as Lorong East Coast before being renamed after Cheong Koon Seng in 1934. Koon Seng, one of the first 13 students of Anglo-Chinese School, built the Theatre Royal and Star Opera Company in 1870s

Koon Seng Road was a predominantly residental area, but ithad a vibrant business atmosphere as there were shophouses along the road. The road was also a Peranakan enclave in the past unitl 1970s when the Peranakans began moving to other parts of Singapore.
