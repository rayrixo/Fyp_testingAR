Kim Choo Gallery

Kim choo Kueh chang's Nonya rice dumplings have been around since 1945. Many covet its rich tast, finely woven around the humble delicacy. Today Kim Choo offers a wide range of products and services.

They are now seeking to revatalise the Peranakan heritage. As one of Singapore's oldest heritage family business, they have been tasked to manage a Singapore visitor centre for the precinct of Katong and joo chiat along 111 East coast road
