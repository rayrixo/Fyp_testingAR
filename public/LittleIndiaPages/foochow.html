Former Kandang Kerbau Hospital

Built in 1860 to function as a general hospital, this hospital has witnessed the births of more Singaporeans than any other hospital on the island. The term of Kandang Kerbau (buffalo enclosure) is a reminder of the buffalos that were kept here a long time ago.

In 1997 the government decided to modernise KK hospital and hence relocated the hospital to a new building on the other side of Kampong Java Road. It was then renamed to KK Women's and Children's hopsital.
