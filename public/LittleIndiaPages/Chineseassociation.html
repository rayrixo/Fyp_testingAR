Chinese Clan Association

At Race Course Lane, you will encounter a number of Chinese Clan associations which were established to provide social and economic support or chinese immigrants from the same clans.

Established in 1936, the Singapore Tai Guan Ong See Association was initially located at Jalan Besar. It moved to a three-storey building at Race Course Lane in 1954. At this time, the association provided its memebers a space for socialising and organised activities to encourage interaction and leadning amongst its members.

The association established a mutual aid department in 1962 and began providing welfare services in 1991. Today it also organises annual ceremonies to worship common acestors of its members.
