Sri Veeramakaliamman Temple

Sri Veeramakaliamman is one of the oldest temples in Singapore. Built by Indian pioneers who came to work and live here the temple was the first in the seragoon area and became a foucs of early Indian Social Cultural activities. This outlined the settlement as an indian settlement and developed the area as a indian sector.

As the population grew "Singapore's Little India began to attract more Indians from the nearby areas. Most of the indians which settled in serrangoon were involved in cattle - related activites.
