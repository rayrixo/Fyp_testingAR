Former Kandang Kerbau Hospital

Built in 1915, tekka market was one of the most prominent landmarks along serrangoon Road during the first half of the 20th century. It was also known as Kandang Kerbau Market dut to the predominance of cattle sheds in the area

By the 1930s, Tekka market had become very popular for its wide variety of fresh meat, vegetables and seafood. It was frequented by the working class people hence being known as the peopl's markert.

When it was demolish in 1982, most of the store-holders relocated to the new Zhujiao Market across the street, the new name beinga hanyu pinyin rendition of Tekka. But it was later renamed to tekka center in 2000 as the name Zhujia bore little resembelance to its old name.
